Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is the best type of pillow for side sleepers?
Side sleepers typically benefit from firmer pillows that provide adequate support for the neck and spine.
How often should I replace my pillow?
It is recommended to replace your pillow every 1-2 years, depending on the quality and condition of the pillow.
Can I wash my pillow?
Most pillows can be washed, but it is important to check the care instructions on the pillow's tag before washing.
What is the difference between down and memory foam pillows?
Down pillows are filled with feathers, while memory foam pillows are made of a viscoelastic material that molds to the shape of your head and neck.
How do I know if my pillow is causing me neck pain?
If you wake up with neck pain or stiffness, it may be a sign that your pillow is not providing adequate support for your neck and spine.
